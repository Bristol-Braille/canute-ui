Help:

The machine is viewing a book.

The text of the book appears line by line on the display.

The selection buttons next to the text are inactive.

The last line displays the current book title and the current and total number of pages in the book.

Short pressing the selection button beside the last line opens a quick access menu to navigate the library.

Long pressing the selection button beside the last line opens the library menu.

Short pressing the left and right navigation arrows move through the book pages (if more than one page).

Long pressing the left and right navigation arrows moves to the previous and next chapters in the book respectively.

Short pressing the home button does opens the library.

Long pressing the home button opens the book menu.
